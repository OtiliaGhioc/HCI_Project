Detailed Description

Elizabeth is a old lady who has problems in detecting how a keyboard/ touch screen system works.

She needs help when it comes to following moving objects on a screen and interacting with them.

Scenarios
  1. She is old and need more practice with technologies such as smartphones or computers.
  2. She went through a complicated operation that affected her locomotor system.
  3. She suffered an accident that damaged her locomotor system.
  4. She has problems following fast moving objects.

She needs help when it comes to recognizing which move he truly wanted to make.

Scenarios
  1. He has parkinson's which makes his finger tremble on the screen/ keyboard.
