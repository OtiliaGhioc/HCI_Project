Detailed Description

Emily Thompson, an engineer at ABC Technologies, is a primary persona for our application. She was born on October 23, 1990, and currently resides in Iasi, Romania. Emily attended Palo Alto High School and has a moderate level of technological proficiency. She enjoys puzzle and strategy games and is seeking an app that provides a relaxing break during her busy work schedule.


Emily values simplicity in user interfaces and prefers applications that offer offline functionality. Customizable colors or visually appealing interfaces enhance her gaming experience. As an engineer, Emily works in a technical environment and appreciates applications that engage her problem-solving skills.


Her goal with the app is to have fun and relax during her free time while improving her dexterity and eye-hand coordination. She wants to feel productive and accomplished, even during breaks. Emily does not mention any specific disabilities relevant to our research.

Detailed Description

Alex Parker, a 16-year-old boy from Seattle, is determined to overcome the physical limitations imposed by a tragic accident. Despite losing his arms and being paralyzed, Alex has a strong passion for gaming and wants to enjoy it like any other teenager. He seeks games with voice input compatibility, simple interfaces, and easy-to-use user flows.


Alex's primary goals are to have fun, relax, and improve his strategic thinking through accessible gaming experiences.


Although he faces challenges in finding suitable games and navigating complex interfaces, Alex remains resilient and actively seeks ways to connect with fellow gamers who share similar challenges.
