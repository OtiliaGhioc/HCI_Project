Detailed Description

Mia Johnson is an individual who enjoys spending her time playing video games and prefers a more solitary lifestyle. She finds joy and entertainment within the confines of her home, engaging in gaming activities to have fun and improve her dexterity.


Mia's color blindness and fear of colors influence her gaming preferences, as she seeks out monochromatic or black-and-white games that alleviate any discomfort and allow her to fully immerse herself in the gaming experience.


Her game choices primarily revolve around strategy and puzzle genres, as they provide mental stimulation and rewarding challenges.

Scenarios

In her user journey, Mia interacts with gaming apps like any other user, navigating menus and options comfortably. However, for an optimal experience, she would greatly benefit from a game mode selection option within the app's settings. This feature would allow her to choose between monochromatic or black-and-white visuals, ensuring she can fully enjoy the game without any color-related issues.
